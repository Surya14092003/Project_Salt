Ionised Powdered Salt

Flour Salt

Flour Salts are the Powdered Salts which are a unique type of Sodium chloride(NaCl) that has been grounded to produce the smallest and purset granulations of salt crystals.Compared to regular granulated table salt, its fine-powdered crystal size provides for greater solubility, adhesion, and blendability. A cracker coated with flour salt, for instance, can have a quicker burst of flavor when you bite into it than a cracker sprinkled with coarse salt. This is due to the salt crystal's quicker solubility, which will allow it to dissolve on your tongue more quickly.
